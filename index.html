About Me

My path into tech has not been traditional, but it has followed a clear thread throughout my career.

Before moving into tech, I spent 19 years working as a plumbing and heating technician, troubleshooting complex systems and solving practical problems.

My interest in analysis and decision making later led me into systematic trading, where I became increasingly data-driven, testing ideas, analysing information and evaluating results over time.

That interest eventually led me to the technology behind the tools I was using. The more I worked with data, the more interested I became in software development, data analytics and data engineering.

Today I build projects using Python, SQL, Pandas, PySpark and Django, with a particular interest in data pipelines, analytics, machine learning and financial technology.
